Chainsaw Man

Manga, 100 Capítulos
Denji is a teenage boy living with a Chainsaw Devil named Pochita. Due to the debt his father left behind, he has been living a rock-bottom life while repaying his debt by harvesting devil corpses with Pochita. One day, Denji is betrayed and killed. As his consciousness fades, he makes a contract with Pochita and gets revived as "Chainsaw Man" — a man with a devil's heart. Denji es un joven atrapado en la pobreza, que trabaja para saldar la deuda de su padre fallecido con la Yakuza trabajando como cazador de demonios, con la ayuda de Pochita, su compañero canino, el Demonio de la Motosierra. Denji es traicionado por los Yakuza, quienes lo matan por un contrato con el DemonioZombi.
